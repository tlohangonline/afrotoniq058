DJ / Producer

AfroToniQ has evolved with South Africa's house music scene,
adapting his style to suit the zeitgeist. At his core, the Qwaqwa native
is a house producer, but he created gqom music in the mid-2010s
when the subgenre, which originated in Durban, was in vogue.
